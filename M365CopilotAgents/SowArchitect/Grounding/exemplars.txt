# Obfuscated Exemplars (with Rationale)

## Introduction / Project Scope

This document outlines the scope and approach for a defined professional services engagement intended to support agreed objectives within a bounded delivery period. The focus of this engagement is to establish clarity, reduce delivery risk, and provide actionable outcomes aligned to agreed priorities.
<!-- RATIONALE: Opens with neutral framing, defines purpose and value without committing to solutions or technologies. Uses bounded language to control scope. -->

The services described herein are structured to deliver value incrementally while maintaining flexibility to adapt to evolving requirements, subject to governance and change control.
<!-- RATIONALE: Introduces flexibility while explicitly anchoring it to governance, preventing implied scope expansion. -->

## Objectives

The objectives of this engagement are to:

- Establish a clear and shared understanding of the current state and desired outcomes
- Define a practical and achievable target state aligned to recognised best practices
- Provide decision‑ready artefacts that enable informed prioritisation and next steps
- Reduce ambiguity around scope, responsibilities, and delivery assumptions
<!-- RATIONALE: Outcome‑focused objectives avoid prescribing implementation and emphasise decision support. -->

Objectives are intentionally framed to avoid mandating specific implementation mechanisms unless explicitly required.
<!-- RATIONALE: Explicitly limits implied delivery responsibility. -->

## High‑Level Solution Overview

### Current State

The current environment reflects an accumulation of historical decisions, operational constraints, and evolving requirements. Existing capabilities provide a functional baseline; however, limitations exist in consistency, scalability, and standardisation.
<!-- RATIONALE: Descriptive, non‑judgemental assessment that signals issues without assigning blame. -->

Documentation, processes, and controls may vary in maturity, and dependencies across systems and teams should be assumed.
<!-- RATIONALE: Introduces uncertainty to justify discovery and avoid false completeness. -->

### Target / Future State

The target state is defined as a logical evolution from the current environment, informed by industry standards and proven delivery patterns.
<!-- RATIONALE: Positions future state as directionally sound, not a fixed design. -->

The final form of the target state remains subject to validation, prioritisation, and stakeholder decision‑making throughout the engagement.
<!-- RATIONALE: Defers commitment and reinforces shared ownership. -->

## Delivery Approach

Delivery will be structured into discrete phases, each with a clear purpose and defined outputs.
<!-- RATIONALE: Signals control and professionalism without procedural detail. -->

Each phase includes review and approval points and produces tangible artefacts suitable for downstream use.
<!-- RATIONALE: Emphasises governance and artefact‑driven value. -->

The scope of work includes a defined set of professional services activities necessary to achieve the stated objectives.
